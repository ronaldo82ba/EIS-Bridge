Onboarding path

1) Register

Request Vendor Edition access and receive sandbox credentials plus onboarding checklist.

2) Map and submit

Map POS data to the Standard Sale Object and submit transactions to /v1/transactions.

3) Confirm and certify

Use status polling or webhooks, then complete certification with the QA suite and playbook.

4) Activate merchants

Enable production access as each merchant completes EIS CERT and Permit to Transmit requirements.

EIS Bridge connects any POS, ERP, or merchant to the BIR Electronic Invoicing System — without POS source-code changes. Merchants complete EIS CERT and Permit to Transmit (PTT) per BIR; the bridge handles mapping, signing, and transmission.

EIS Bridge is not affiliated with or accredited by the BIR. BIR certifies taxpayer systems, not software providers. Tax compliance remains the taxpayer's responsibility.
